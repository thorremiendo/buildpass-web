About Us
Vision
”God-fearing organization, leader in providing quality service ensuring that all buildings and structures in the City are consistent with sound engineering and architectural principles and environmental protection and preservation, towards the City’s Vision; Baguio as a Tourism Oriented City and Center of Education.”
Mission
“We shall render quality of service to conscientiously implement all laws, rules and regulation to ensure safety of buildings, and to protect and preserve the environment.”
Mandate
  1. Initiates, reviews and recommends changes in policies and objectives, plans and programs, techniques, procedures practices, and guidelines on vertical infrastructure development and other related public works for the City.
  2. Administers coordinated supervises and controls the construction, maintenance, improvement and repair of vertical infrastructure development and other related public works (i.e. signages and billboards, fences etc.) in the City.
  3. Prepares detailed engineering plans for vertical infrastructure development projects and other related public works for the City.
  4. Participated in the planning of special projects for the city and implement components within its scope of responsibility.
  5. Manages and maintains all city government-owned vertical structures and other facilities and structures i.e. hydro plant that may be assigned by the City Government from time to time.
  6. Exercise such other powers and performs other functions and duties as may be prescribed by law or ordinance or assigned by the City Government from time to time.
